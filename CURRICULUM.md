## Study Recommendations

### Daily Study Routine
1. **Theory (30-40%)** - Read and understand concepts
2. **Code Review (20-30%)** - Study implementations
3. **Practice (40-50%)** - Solve problems

### Weekly Goals
- Complete 1-3 topics depending on complexity
- Solve minimum 5-10 problems per topic
- Review previous week's topics

### Monthly Review
- Revisit all topics from the month
- Solve mixed problem sets
- Identify weak areas and reinforce

### Before Moving to Next Phase
- Complete all topics in current phase
- Achieve 80%+ success rate on medium problems
- Solve at least 2-3 hard problems from the phase
